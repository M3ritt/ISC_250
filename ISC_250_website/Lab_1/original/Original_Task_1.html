Brownie Recipe

Recipe Data

Yield 16 servings
Prep time 10 minutes
Cook time 30 minutes
Ready 60 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup of butter
  • 2 Oz Unsweetened chocolate
  • 1 Cup of sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 Large eggs
  • 3/4 All-purpose clour (sifted)
  • 1/2 Cup of Chopped Walnuts

Cooking directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 Degrees F
  2. In medium saucepan, melt butter and chocolate
  3. Remove from heat, stir in sugar
  4. Fold in eggs, carefully one at a time.
  5. Add vanilla, stir in flour and nuts, mix well.
  6. Spread mixture into greased pan, bake at 350 for 30 minutes, let cool, cut and serve!
